## ü§ù On Communities and Project Privacy

DealeGear **does not encourage** an open community for project sharing.  
Personal projects often contain strategic and sensitive details, and sharing them too early can be harmful.

Therefore, the platform prioritizes:

- Privacy
- Autonomy
- Guided individual development

We maintain a presence on social networks, but **there is no open community requirement**.
